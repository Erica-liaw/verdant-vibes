About Us

Verdant Vibe is a Singapore-based plant shop catering to millennials who love beautiful spaces but want low-maintenance greenery. Whether you're living in a cozy apartment or setting up a stylish office corner, our plants are chosen for their ease of care and stunning aesthetics.

We also offer aesthetic pots and curated shelves to complete your plant setup—all with a strong commitment to sustainability.

Shop

Visit our physical store in the heart of Singapore or browse online. We offer nationwide delivery with no minimum order. A flat delivery fee applies.

Our website helps you match plants to your space and lifestyle with our smart plant-matching tool.
